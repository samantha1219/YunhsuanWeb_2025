CONCEPT

For over a century, Hsinchu Dongmen Market has been a culturally and artistically significant landmark in Hsinchu. Despite undergoing various reforms, it continues to serve as a testament to the current era. "Soundscape Mapping: Hsinchu Dongmen Market" is an interactive auditory project that documents the market's memories.

This project allows audiences to freely explore the market and interact with various objects through an interactive sound map. The market's cultural and auditory value is enhanced by its unique soundscape, created through rhythmic fragmentation of actual market sounds, and background music.
