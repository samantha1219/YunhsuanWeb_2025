CONCEPT

"Euphoria" is an interactive kinetic installation that explores the complex relationship between technology and human emotion. Through responsive digital projections and motion-sensitive interfaces, the artwork creates an immersive environment where participants' movements directly influence the visual and kinetic elements of the piece.

The installation investigates how digital technology can amplify and reflect human emotional states, creating a feedback loop between the participant and the technological system. By combining projection mapping with Arduino-controlled kinetic elements, "Euphoria" questions the boundaries between physical and digital experiences, inviting viewers to explore their relationship with responsive environments and examine how technology might serve as an extension of human expression and feeling.
