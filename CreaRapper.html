CreaRapper

Real-time Interactive Rap Generator

2021-01 Interactive Music Tech

Technology / Programming | YUN-HSUAN CHENG

The real-time interactive rap generator powered by Python connects physical devices to the digital realm. This innovative endeavor aims to democratize the creative process in independent music production, creating an accessible platform that enables individuals to actively engage in musical composition.

Technologies

Python Text-to-Speech Microcomputer Real-time Audio

CONCEPT

In today's landscape of music creation, everyone can be a creator, contributing to a rich tapestry of artistic expression. Our goal is to create an accessible and user-friendly platform that enables individuals to actively engage in musical composition and receive instant, dynamically generated works of art.

We leverage microcomputer technology and Python's advanced text-to-speech capabilities to achieve this. This fusion of technology forms an interactive medium that encourages collaborative artistic ventures, blurring the lines between creators and their audiences.

TECHNICAL INNOVATION

Our initiative embraces the inclusive spirit of contemporary creativity, offering an immersive and transformative experience. By combining microcomputers and text-to-speech technology, users can articulate their creative visions with unprecedented immediacy.

This collaboration between human ingenuity and cutting-edge technology transforms artistic ideas into tangible realities. Our project seeks to bridge the gap between creative expression and technological innovation, fostering collaboration and empowering a new generation of creators.
